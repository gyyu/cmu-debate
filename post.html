Swarthmore Novice

We sent four teams to Swarthmore Novice and had amazing results! Here were our awesome participants:

  • Whisker Wars: Grace Yu and Juan Paniagua
  • The Dudesons: Keya Ganatra and Jeffrey Ko
  • Real Sidechicks: Tom An and Akshina Gupta
  • Married at First Sight: Kyle Coopersmith and Jacob Love (from Pitt)
Congratulations to Whisker Wars for going 4-1! Tab cards can be found here (Flight A) and here (Flight B).

In-House Tournament

We hosted our first-ever in-house tournament with Pitt! The entire tournament went amazingly smooth, and thanks so much to Alison Chan for running tab and dealing with all of the last-minute drops. We're aiming to collaborate more with Pitt in this upcoming year, so the team bonding that took place was great! Congratulations to the following top speakers:

  • First Speaker: Rhea Jain
  • Second Speaker: Hannah Sun
  • Third Speaker: Komal Dhull
  • Fourth Speaker: Jake Love
  • Fifth Speaker: Nate Kindja
Additionally, congratulations to our semifinalists (CMU Hannah/Evans, Pitt Kyle/Jake), our Finalist (Hybrid Sam/Marie), and our champion -- CMU Rhea/Komal! We were amazed by the eloquence and sheer skill all of you demonstrated, and thanks again for participating!
